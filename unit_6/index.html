about

My name is Jackie. I'm an aspiring UX designer and web designer. A complete career change has brought me to take General Assembly's HTML/CSS and Web Design class, continuing on to their User Experience Design course. From a young age, I've always been keen on good design and color, and appreciate the beauty in simplicity. Put simply, I know and love what looks good.

I've always been interested in creating beautiful things via crocheting, knitting, or crafting with cardstock paper, glitter, and pens. Substitute the crochet needle and scissors for my MacBook, headphones, and a good eye for design and here we are.

I love to exercise my creative muscles. Have a look at my portfolio. Let's chat.
